About Us

Govt. Medical Institute Kazipur is a well-known healthcare education center situated in Kazipur, Sirajganj. Since its establishment in 2017, the institute has been committed to producing skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals to serve the community and the nation.

The institute currently offers the Medical Assistant Training Course (MATS), designed to provide students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary to succeed in the medical field. Our experienced faculty members and well-structured academic programs ensure quality education and hands-on clinical training for every student.

One of the highlights of Govt. Medical Institute Kazipur is its friendly and supportive campus environment.

Our mission is to build responsible, capable, and caring healthcare providers who will contribute to a healthier, better society.

Course Information

  • Course Neme: Medical Assistant Training Course
  • Duration: 4 Years
  • Internship: 6 Months
